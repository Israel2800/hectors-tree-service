# Hector's Tree Service QR Rollout Plan

## Objective

Use a premium branded QR code that sends traffic directly to:

`https://www.hectors-treeservice.com`

The goal is to turn offline impressions into website visits, estimate requests, and stronger brand recall.

## Recommended QR Versions

- Primary recommendation:
  [hectors-home-qr-tree-logo-premium.png](/D:/Freelance/hectors-tree-service/marketing/qr-codes/hectors-home-qr-tree-logo-premium.png)
  Best for trucks, yard signs, uniforms, invoices, and general print because the circular tree mark stays clear even at smaller sizes.

- Secondary recommendation:
  [hectors-home-qr-full-logo-premium.png](/D:/Freelance/hectors-tree-service/marketing/qr-codes/hectors-home-qr-full-logo-premium.png)
  Best for business cards, brochures, and social graphics where stronger branding is more important and there is enough print space.

## Placement Strategy

- Trucks and trailers:
  Use the tree-logo version. Keep the QR in a clean white area with strong contrast and enough whitespace around it.

- Business cards:
  Use either version, but the full-logo version works especially well when the card already has room and the design is clean.

- Door hangers, flyers, postcards:
  Use the tree-logo version unless the layout is very spacious.

- Social media graphics:
  Use the full-logo version if the QR appears large on the design.

- Invoices, estimates, and printed documents:
  Use the tree-logo version for reliability and faster scanning.

## Size Guidance

- Business card minimum:
  1.0 in to 1.25 in wide

- Flyers and postcards:
  1.5 in to 2.0 in wide

- Truck decals:
  4 in to 8 in wide depending on viewing distance

- Yard signs:
  2.5 in to 4 in wide

## Best Practices

- Always keep a quiet white margin around the QR.
- Do not stretch the QR non-proportionally.
- Do not place the QR over gradients, photos, or busy textures.
- Print from the high-resolution PNG file.
- Test the printed QR from different phones before final large-batch printing.

## Recommended First Rollout

1. Put the tree-logo QR on trucks and trailers.
2. Add the tree-logo QR to business cards and estimates.
3. Use the full-logo QR in marketing graphics and premium print pieces.
4. Track whether more customers say they found the company through the website or scanned from a truck/sign.
